# Hom Nay An Gi? - User Guide

## Gioi thieu

**"Hom Nay An Gi?"** la ung dung giup gia dinh Viet quyet dinh an gi nhanh nhat trong 10 giay.
Khong can tranh luan, khong can suy nghi nhieu - chi can 1 click!

---

## Cac tinh nang chinh

### 1. Goi y thong minh (Trang chu)

- Bam nut **"Hom nay an gi?"** de nhan 3 goi y mon an
- App tu dong goi y theo buoi (sang/trua/toi)
- Bam vao mon an de chon
- Bam **"Goi y khac"** neu khong thich

### 2. Random nhanh

- Bam nut **"Random nhanh 1 mon"** de duoc chon ngau nhien 1 mon
- Phu hop khi ban khong muon suy nghi gi ca

### 3. An sang (Tab An sang)

App co 3 loai goi y buoi sang:
- **Nau nhanh**: Mon nau tai nha duoi 15 phut (trung chien, chao, mi goi...)
- **An ngoai**: Mon mua ngoai duong (pho, bun bo, banh mi, xoi...)
- **Tien loi**: Mon mua o cua hang tien loi (Ministop, GS25, Circle K...)

### 4. Nguyen lieu co gi nau do (Tab Nguyen lieu)

- Nhap nguyen lieu ban co trong tu lanh
- Chon nhanh tu danh sach nguyen lieu pho bien
- Bam **"Tim mon nau duoc"** de xem goi y
- App se goi y mon phu hop voi nguyen lieu cua ban

### 5. Vote gia dinh (Tab Vote)

- Nhap ten thanh vien gia dinh (it nhat 2 nguoi)
- Bam **"Bat dau Vote"**
- Moi nguoi lan luot vote: Thich / Khong cho moi mon
- App tu dong chon mon co diem cao nhat

---

## Cach su dung

### Tren dien thoai (PWA)

1. Mo trinh duyet va truy cap dia chi app
2. Tren iOS: Bam "Share" > "Add to Home Screen"
3. Tren Android: Bam menu 3 cham > "Add to Home Screen"
4. Mo app tu man hinh chinh nhu ung dung binh thuong

### Tren may tinh

1. Mo trinh duyet va truy cap dia chi app
2. Su dung nhu trang web binh thuong

---

## Meo su dung

- **Buoi sang ban ron?** -> Dung tab "An sang" de chon nhanh
- **Khong biet nau gi?** -> Nhap nguyen lieu co san vao tab "Nguyen lieu"
- **Ca nha khong thong nhat?** -> Dung tinh nang "Vote gia dinh"
- **Luoi suy nghi?** -> Bam "Random nhanh 1 mon"
